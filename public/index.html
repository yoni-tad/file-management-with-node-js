FAQ

Any Questions? Look Here

Have questions about FileFrame? We've compiled answers to the most common queries to help you understand our file management solution better.

What is FileFrame and how does it work?

FileFrame is a file management platform designed to help you organize, store, and access your files securely and efficiently. You can easily upload files, create folders, share with others, and search for documents quickly—all from a user-friendly interface.

How secure is my data with FileFrame?

Your data security is our top priority. FileFrame uses advanced encryption methods to protect your files and personal information. Our secure cloud storage ensures that your data is safe from unauthorized access.

How do I recover deleted files?

FileFrame includes a robust backup and recovery feature. If you accidentally delete a file, you can restore it from the backup within 30 days. After that, the files will be permanently deleted for security reasons.

What support is available if I need help?

With your lifetime payment, you receive priority customer support. Our support team is available via email and chat to assist with any questions or issues you may have.
